About

Kevin P. McClain, PhD

I am a Senior GIS Analyst and physical geographer with a doctorate in Earth Science from Istanbul Technical University. My work spans municipal GIS, geoscientific illustration, and cartography, with a particular interest in how maps can make complex spatial information legible to both specialists and non-specialists. I also started painting recently.

I am currently based in Arizona but hopefully not forever. 11 years in Europe radicalized me to appreciate vibrant cities, which we do not have here. I also do not want to continue experiencing climate change in an extreme climate like the Sonoran Desert.
