Overview

My first duty at the Town of Marana was to update the reference map for the town. I went with a plain phonebook-like map that attempted to show the basic features of a city. The map was used on walls in different departments at the town and was on the website.

2022 Town of Marana reference map. Distributed in print and on the town website.

Municipal complex facilities map. A detailed view of the town municipal campus and directions from the freeway.
